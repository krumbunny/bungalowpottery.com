Bungalow Pottery is the ceramics studio of Ken Chin-Purcell, and is located among the Milton Square shops of Saint Anthony Park in St. Paul, MN.

It is both a pottery and a shop that is open for purchases while I am working.

My work is made from white stoneware and is thrown and high fired at the studio. My goal is to make pots that you can give or use with pleasure: expressive and beautiful.
