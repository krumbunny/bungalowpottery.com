Directions

Bungalow Pottery is located in Saint Paul, MN at the corner of Como Ave. and Carter Ave in a 100 year old complex of shops called Milton Square.

Nearby landmarks are Muffuletta Cafe, the Como Ave. Bibelot store and the St. Paul campus of the University of Minnesota.

Also in Milton Square is The Little Wine Shoppe, Micawber's Books, Lady Elegant's Tea Shop and Satori Photography.

Across Carter Ave. is Finish Bistro (a cafe and bakery) and Dunn Bros. coffee shop. On the other side of Como Ave. is our branch library.

There is ample parking for visitors behind Milton Square.
